FL Studios Tutorial Description

Welcome to the Fl Studios Tutorial site! On this website, we will be guiding you through the basics of the program in order for you to be able to create your own music. Our goal is for you to understand the mechanics of the program in the simplest way possible. We will be walking you through the different plug-ins and VST's of the program and how to use them to your advantage. We have also provided various sources to different plug-ins and VST's that we find beneficial to the music making process. We hope you find these tutorials to be simple and that you have fun during the process!

What is a Plug-in?

A plug-in is a "self-contained" piece of code" that is used within the FL Studios. These plug-ins are used to enhance the functionalities of the sounds that you are producing. Simply put, they help to adjust various functions such as dynamic range, reverberation, dely, and more.

What is a VST?

A VST is a type of plug-in that produces the desired soudns that you are looking for. They produce the audio to your music, and replicate a numerous amount of instruments.
