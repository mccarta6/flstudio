Ominsphere 2

Price: $499

Omnisphere 2 is considered to be one of the most popular VST's out there. Many Hip Hop and Rap producers use this VST to create their beats. While it is great for this genre, it includes a variety of different instruments that can be used for any genre. There are over 7,000 presets included with the program that you can manipulate to get the sound that you desire. One great feature of this VST is that you can soundmatch any sound that you like to find other similar and unique sounds. This is a great VST to start off with once you have mastered the other VST's included with FL Studios.

Kontakt 6

Price: $399

Kontakt 6 is one of the best piano VST's out there. While it is widely known for its piano presets, it includes many other sounds such as symphony orchestras, drums, and a variety of other instruments. Kontakt 6 includes around 1,000 instruments and it will greatly help to add to the desired sounds that you are looking for.

Scaler 2

Price: $60

While Scaler 2 doesn't offer many sounds with it, there is an enormous benefit with this VST. This program includes built-in chords that are categorized by the key that they are in, as well as the mood of the chords that are provided. This can prove to be very helpful when you have writers block and are not sure where to start. This feature undoubtable makes up for the lack of sounds that it provides. Even still, this VST includes a greatly sounding piano that you are able to modify to your liking.
