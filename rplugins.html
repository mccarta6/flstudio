iZotope Vinyl

Price: Free

iZotope Vinyl is useful when you want to make your sounds unique and sound as if you are actually playing an instrument. It can create scratches, warps, and mechanical noises that real instruments produce when played.

CamelCrusher

Price: Free

CamelCrusher is a great tool to create colorful are warm effects to your sounds. It offers two distortion sounds which can be blended together to creatue a variety of different textures to your sounds. It also offers an 'amount' knob that allows you to choose how vibrant you want your sound to be.

Guitar Rig 6 Pro

Price: $199

Guitar Rid 6 is a great plug-in when using guitar instruments. This plug-in will help to make you guitars sound clean and as realistic as possible. It offers many features such as being able to amplify, distory, modulate, and many other ways to create a unique sound.
